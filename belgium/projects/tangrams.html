Tangrams Teacher's Resource

Tangrams are geometric puzzles that originated in ancient China. The objective is to solve the puzzle with the geometric shapes that are provided. Often the shapes have to be rotated to fit into the tangram. This is an excellent exercise in visualization and spatial skill.


Grades: Kindergarten and up
Type: Short to Medium-term

Children can generate up to 60 tangrams to solve. Many are very difficult to solve so students may use trial and error. Most children find these puzzles very engrossing.


Grades: 6 to 9
Type: Long-term

Students can program their own picture-maker to create tangram-like puzzles. They do not have to limit themselves to the strict tangram shapes in the project. They can play with the geometric shapes to create new picture puzzles to solve.
