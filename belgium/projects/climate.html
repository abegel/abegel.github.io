World Climate Map Teacher's Resource

Weather forecasting is a science and an art. To be a good weather forecaster, you must know some meteorology and be able to make smart predictions based on experience. Temperature actually measures the speed of air molecules. The faster air molecules move, the warmer it is; the slower they move, the colder the air is. Climate is the daily and seasonal weather that a particular region experiences over a period of time.


Grades: 3 to 5
Type: Short-term

Students can examine the map and then use it as the basis for discussion about the climate and what determines it. The climate in January shows the extreme differences in temperature all over the world. Ask how a climate map in July would compare.


Grades: 3 to 5
Type: Medium-term

Students can make a weather map of the country or region they live in. Import the map and use turtle shapes for the weather conditions. Program the turtles to produce sounds relating to the weather.
