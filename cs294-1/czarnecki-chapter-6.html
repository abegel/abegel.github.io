Czarnecki Chapter 6.

March 6, 2002

 

I didn’t learn anything new about polymorphism in this chapter, though I have to say, it’s probably a great introduction to it for an undergraduate class. It is very concrete, with quite a few examples. Perhaps Alex and George might use this in CS164 next year. I am quite impressed as well with the authors’ ability to talk about C++ and the STL without any evidence of disdain. I imagine that most programming language experts would not have been able to do that.
